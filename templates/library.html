Library

Knowledge Hub for Every Learner at Subhash Academy

Our library supports BCA, PGDCA and DCA students with quality books, journals, previous year papers, digital references and a quiet reading environment.

Academic Collection

Topic-wise collection for programming, databases, networking, office tools, communication skills and competitive exam preparation.

Reference Material

University syllabus books, practical manuals, project guidance material and previous year papers for exam-focused preparation.

Reading Zone

Calm study space with guided support from faculty for doubt-solving, revision planning and productive self-study sessions.

Computer Lab

Practical learning with modern systems

Our lab is designed for hands-on practice in programming, database management, office tools, and project development. Students get regular practical sessions with faculty guidance.

  • High-performance computers for coding and software practice
  • Internet-enabled systems for research and e-learning
  • Lab support for BCA, PGDCA and DCA practical curriculum
  • Project and presentation preparation support

Library Timings

Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM. During exams, reading room support is extended as per the college notice.

Issue and Return

Students can issue books with valid ID card. Standard issue period is 7 days and can be renewed if the book is available.

Digital Support

E-notes, question banks, and practical references are provided for BCA, PGDCA and DCA students through guided faculty support.

Library Facilities

Resources that help students perform better

  • Updated semester-wise technical books for all courses
  • Career guidance books and interview preparation material
  • Daily newspaper and magazine corner for current affairs
  • Faculty-recommended reading lists for each semester
  • Support for project reports, assignments and presentations
Library Rules

Simple guidelines for a better study environment

  • Maintain silence inside the reading area.
  • Handle books and journals carefully; avoid marking pages.
  • Carry student ID card while issuing or returning books.
  • Return books on time to avoid delay penalties.
  • Keep mobile phones on silent mode inside the library.
