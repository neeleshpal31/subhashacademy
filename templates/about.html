About Us

Subhash Academy (College of IT & Management)

Subhash Academy is an AICTE-approved institute and a leading institution specializing in professional courses such as Computer Management and IT disciplines. We are recognized for our excellence in education and are one of the largest colleges for professional education in the Kannauj and Farrukhabad districts. The institute is affiliated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University of Journalism & Communication, Bhopal, a renowned institution known for its academic excellence and industry-aligned curriculum. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a commitment to innovation, we provide a dynamic and enriching learning environment that empowers students to succeed in their careers since 1998.
