About Us

Subhash Academy (College of IT & Management)

Subhash Academy is a leading institution for computer, management, and professional education in Kannauj district. The institute is approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi) and associated with Makhanlal Chaturvedi National University of Journalism and Communication, Bhopal. We have been offering professional programs since 1998, including DCA, BCA, and PGDCA.
